# Option Buying Strategies

## Strategy 1: Long Call Setup

**Conditions**:
- Futures: Price up > 1% + OI increase > 5%
- Options: Call OI decreasing at ATM strikes + Put OI increasing
- Recommendation: BUY ATM CALL

## Strategy 2: Long Put Setup

**Conditions**:
- Futures: Price down > 1% + OI increase > 5%  
- Options: Put OI decreasing at ATM strikes + Call OI increasing
- Recommendation: BUY ATM PUT

## Strategy 3: Breakout Setup

**Conditions**:
- Futures: Strong price move (>1.5%) + High volume + OI increase
- Options: Volume spike in relevant options
- Recommendation: BUY OTM CALL/PUT

## Strategy 4: Max Pain Setup

**Conditions**:
- Current price > 2% above max pain → Favor calls
- Current price < 2% below max pain → Favor puts
- Recommendation: BUY CALL/PUT based on max pain position
